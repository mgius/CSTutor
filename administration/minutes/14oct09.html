Discussion with Professor Fisher:

Include a functionality to show, after taking a quiz, what the instructor's expectation is for your quiz score The student can't see other students' statistics.

Professor wants a form of built in chat and remote access so he can control student's screen.

We decided to have an instructor client and a student client.

Student must login or use anonymous option however, the Instructor client will allow for offline work in order to create lessons.
