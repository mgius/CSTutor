2.3.5 Previewing a Lesson

This scenario shows how the instructor can preview a lesson. Previewing lessons is important because it allows the instructor to proofread the lesson and look at the layout of the lesson. In this scenario, we assume that the instructor has just logged into the appropriate class and is looking at the lesson editor page. Details of logging in as an instructor can be found in Section 2.1.

To preview a lesson, the instructor selects the Preview/Save button on the toolbar in the lesson editor. Then, the instructor will be sent to a preview page that will been displayed exactly as how a student will see the page. Details of browsing the student pages can be found at Section 2.4.
