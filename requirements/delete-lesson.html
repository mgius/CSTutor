2.3.1.4 Deleting a Class/Lesson

If an instructor wants to delete a lesson or class, they have the option to do so under the "Options" section.

FIXME
The instructor will begin by finding the lesson to be deleted by browsing through the navigation bar. The instructor will then click on the Lesson or Class to view it, then click "Delete". A simple "Are you sure?" box will pop up with a "Yes" and "No" button. Clicking "Yes" will delete the lesson or class. "No" will ignore the delete request.

Deleting a class will delete all contents inside. So if the instructor were to delete a class containing 30 Lessons. The class and all 30 lessons will be deleted.
